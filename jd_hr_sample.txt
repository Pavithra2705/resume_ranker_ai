JOB TITLE: Senior Hiring Manager – Technology Division
COMPANY: NovaTech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
LOCATION: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)
EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: 6–10 years
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-Time

─────────────────────────────────────────────
ABOUT THE COMPANY
─────────────────────────────────────────────
NovaTech Solutions is a fast-growing technology company with 3,000+ employees across
12 countries. We build enterprise SaaS products used by Fortune 500 companies. Our
People & Talent team is at the core of our growth strategy — we hire 400+ tech
professionals annually and are expanding rapidly into AI and cloud infrastructure.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
ABOUT THE ROLE
─────────────────────────────────────────────
We are looking for a Senior Hiring Manager to lead full-cycle technical recruitment
for our Engineering, Data Science, and Product divisions. You will own the hiring
strategy for 3 business units, manage a team of 5 recruiters, and directly partner
with C-level and VP-level stakeholders to shape workforce planning. This is a
high-impact, high-visibility role that directly influences company growth.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
REQUIRED SKILLS
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- Full-cycle technical recruitment (sourcing to offer)
- Team leadership and recruiter mentoring (team of 3–8)
- Stakeholder management with VP/CXO-level leaders
- Workforce planning and headcount forecasting
- Applicant Tracking Systems: Greenhouse, Lever, or Workday
- Data-driven hiring: time-to-fill, offer acceptance rate, quality of hire
- Competency-based and behavioral interviewing
- Employer branding and talent pipeline development
- Offer negotiation and compensation benchmarking
- Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) hiring practices

─────────────────────────────────────────────
PREFERRED SKILLS
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- Experience hiring for AI, ML, or Data Science roles
- Familiarity with HackerRank, Codility, or technical assessment platforms
- LinkedIn Recruiter certified
- HR analytics tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- Knowledge of Indian and global labor laws
- SHRM-CP or SHRM-SCP certification

─────────────────────────────────────────────
RESPONSIBILITIES
─────────────────────────────────────────────
- Own end-to-end recruitment for 80–120 annual tech hires across Engineering,
  Data Science, and Product Management divisions
- Lead, coach, and performance-manage a team of 5 junior and mid-level recruiters
- Partner directly with CTOs, VPs of Engineering, and Heads of Product to define
  hiring needs, role profiles, and interview processes
- Build and maintain a warm talent pipeline of 500+ pre-qualified candidates
- Define and track recruiting KPIs: time-to-fill < 28 days, offer acceptance > 85%,
  hiring manager satisfaction > 4.2/5.0
- Design structured interview frameworks and scorecards for 15+ job families
- Lead employer branding initiatives: campus hiring, tech events, LinkedIn campaigns
- Drive DEI hiring targets — 40% diverse slate requirement on all senior roles
- Analyze recruiting funnel data weekly and present insights to CHRO and leadership
- Negotiate complex compensation packages including ESOPs, signing bonuses, and
  relocation for senior technical hires
- Manage external agency partnerships and control agency spend < 8% of total hiring cost

─────────────────────────────────────────────
EDUCATION
─────────────────────────────────────────────
MBA in Human Resources or a Master's degree in Organizational Psychology, Business
Administration, or a related field strongly preferred.
Bachelor's degree in any discipline is the minimum requirement.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
EXPERIENCE
─────────────────────────────────────────────
6 to 10 years of talent acquisition experience, with at least 3 years in a
leadership role managing a team of recruiters. Prior experience in a technology
company or recruiting for technical roles is mandatory.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
COMPENSATION
─────────────────────────────────────────────
CTC: ₹18–28 LPA depending on experience
Benefits: ESOPs, health insurance (family), flexible work hours, ₹1L learning budget
